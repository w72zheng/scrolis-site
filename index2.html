How it works

Not magic.
Just a better habit.

01

Connect your timeline.

Link your social accounts. Scrolis reads your actual follows — not a generic news feed — because your blind spots are specific to you, not the same as everyone else's.

02

We show you three views.

For every story: what your feed thinks, what the broader conversation looks like, and perspectives so far outside your usual circles you'd never find them on your own.

03

Read and explore.

Five stories. When you've read them, there's nothing left to open. No next page, no pull-to-refresh, no algorithm ready to fill the silence.

Most people are reading their network's reaction to the news.

Not the news itself. Not multiple sides of it. The filtered, amplified, engagement-optimised version their algorithm decided they'd respond to. Scrolis was built to fix that quietly, without making you feel bad about it.

What makes it different

Built with a few
firm opinions.

01

Your Feed's Take

See the consensus from accounts you already follow. If every voice in your circle is saying the same thing — you'll know that's what's happening, and that that's all you're getting.

02

Wandering

A slightly wider view. Voices outside your usual orbit, surfaced by relevance — not by what's likely to make you react. Just the broader conversation, as it actually exists.

03

Uncharted — the views your feed almost never surfaces.

Perspectives that rarely reach people like you. Some will be uncomfortable. Some will be wrong. But the ones that make you stop and actually think? Those are worth 90 seconds of your morning.

04

Your Perspective Profile

A breakdown of what you've been seeing and how diverse your information diet actually is. Not to judge — just to see. Most people are quietly surprised by what they find.
