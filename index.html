How it works

Not magic.
Just a better habit.

01

Connect your timeline.

Link your social accounts. Scrolis reads the posts of the people you follow — not a generic news feed — because your blind spots are specific to you, not the same as everyone else's.

02

We show you three views.

For every story: what your feed thinks, what the broader conversation looks like, and perspectives outside your usual circles that you'd never find on your own.

03

Read and explore.

A finite set of stories. When you've read them, there's nothing left to scroll. No algorithm reaching into the silence to pull you back in.

Why we built it this way

Most people are reading their network's reaction to the news.

Not the news. Not every side of it. Just the slice an algorithm decided you'd respond to. Scrolis was built to fix that — quietly, and without judgment.
